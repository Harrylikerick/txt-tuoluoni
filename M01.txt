M01.01卍轮王佛顶一字陀罗尼
namaḥ samanta buddhānāṃ oṃ bhrūṃ

M01.02一切如来部母佛眼陀罗尼
namaḥ sarva tathāgate bhyo rha bhyaḥ samya ksaṃ buddhe bhyaḥ oṃ ruru sphuru jvala tiṣṭha siddha locani sarva artha sadhane svāhā

M01.03卍白伞盖佛顶陀罗尼
namaḥ samanta buddhānāṃ oṃ tathagato ṣṇīṣa ana valokita mūrdhna oṃ mama mama hūṃ ni

M01.04卍光聚佛顶陀罗尼
namaḥ samanta buddhānāṃ oṃ tathagato ṣṇīṣa ana valokite mūrdha teju raśi oṃ jvala jvala dhaka dhaka vidhaka vidhaka dara dara vidara vidara cchinda cchinda bhinda bhinda hūṃ hūṃ phaṭ phaṭ svāhā

M01.05卍高佛顶王陀罗尼
namaḥ samanta buddhānāṃ oṃ jvala jvala dīpya dīpya dīpyo dgato ṣṇīṣa dhuna dhuna hūṃ

M01.06卍胜佛顶王陀罗尼
namaḥ samanta buddhānāṃ oṃ jvala jayo ṣṇīṣa jvala jvala bandha bandha dama dama drūṃ drūṃ drūṃ haḥ hana hūṃ namo bhagavato ṣṇīṣāya oṃ ṭrūṃ bandha svāhā

M01.08摧坏佛顶陀罗尼
namo bhagavato ṣṇīṣāya oṃ viki raṇa dhuna dhuna dhūḥ

M01.09卍摧毁佛顶陀罗尼
namo bhagavato ṣṇīṣāya sarva vighna vidhvaṃ sana karāya ṭroṭaya svāhā

M01.10卍无能胜佛顶陀罗尼
namo bhagavato ṣṇīṣāya sarvatra aparājitāya oṃ dharma rāja bhaṣite mahā vidya sarva artha sadhane svāhā

M01.11卍遍照佛顶陀罗尼
namaḥ samanta buddhānāṃ

M01.12卍轮王佛顶心陀罗尼
namaḥ samanta buddhānāṃ apratihata śasanānāṃ oṃ tathā gato ṣṇīṣa ana valo kita mūrdhne cakra varti hūṃ jvala jvala dhaka dhaka dhuna vidhuna trāsaya mālayo tsadaya hana hana bhañja bhañja aṃ aṃ aḥ aḥ kaḥ kaḥ pruṃ khini pruṃ khini kuṇḍa lini aparājita stra dhariṇi hūṃ hūṃ phaṭ phaṭ svāhā

M01.13卍轮王佛顶随心陀罗尼
namaḥ samanta buddhānāṃ apratihata śasanānāṃ oṃ aparājita dhṛk

M01.14卍如来钵陀罗尼
namaḥ samanta buddhānāṃ oṃ Ioka pala adhiṣṭhita dhare dharaya mahā dubhava buddha pattra svāhā

M01.16卍轮王佛顶祈雨陀罗尼
namaḥ samanta buddhānāṃ oṃ bhrūṃ sarva nāgāva rṣantu devā śca samantena svāhā

M01.17卍轮王佛顶止雨陀罗尼
namaḥ samanta buddhānāṃ oṃ bhrūṃ caturda kṣu nāgānāṃ pravi śāntu asmiṃ kumbhe sarva duṣṭa nāgānāṃ rundhami svāhā saṃ rundhami vāsukī prasukhā nāgā rājānū yemanu buddhānāṃ bhrūṃ caturdi kṣunāgānāṃ praviśa asmiṃ kumbhe sarva duṣṭa nāgā nāṃ ruddhami svāhā namaḥ samanta buddhānāṃ apratihata śāsanānāṃ oṃ tathā gato ṣṇīṣa ana valo kita mūrdha tejo raśi hūṃ jvala javla dhaka dhaka vidhaka vidhaka dara dara vidara vidara cchinda cchi nda bhinda bhinda hūṃ hūṃ sarva duṣṭa nāga varṣā sta mbhaya sphaṭ sphaṭ svāhā
