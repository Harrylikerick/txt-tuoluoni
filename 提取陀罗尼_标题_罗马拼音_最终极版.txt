M01.02 一切如来部母佛眼陀罗尼
namaḥ sarva tathāgate bhyo rha bhyaḥ samya ksaṃ buddhe bhyaḥ oṃ ruru sphuru jvala tiṣṭha siddha locani sarva artha sadhane svāhā

M01.03 卍白伞盖佛顶陀罗尼卍
namaḥ samanta buddhānāṃ oṃ tathagato ṣṇīṣa ana valokita mūrdhna oṃ mama mama hūṃ ni

M01.04 卍光聚佛顶陀罗尼卍
namaḥ samanta buddhānāṃ oṃ tathagato ṣṇīṣa ana valokite mūrdha teju raśi oṃ jvala jvala dhaka dhaka vidhaka vidhaka dara dara vidara vidara cchinda cchinda bhinda bhinda hūṃ hūṃ phaṭ phaṭ svāhā

M01.05 卍高佛顶王陀罗尼卍
namaḥ samanta buddhānāṃ oṃ jvala jvala dīpya dīpya dīpyo dgato ṣṇīṣa dhuna dhuna hūṃ

M01.06 卍胜佛顶王陀罗尼卍
namaḥ samanta buddhānāṃ oṃ jvala jayo ṣṇīṣa jvala jvala bandha bandha dama dama drūṃ drūṃ drūṃ haḥ hana hūṃ

M01.07 卍办事真言(一切佛顶心真言)(办事佛顶陀罗尼)卍
namo bhagavato ṣṇīṣāya oṃ ṭrūṃ bandha svāhā

M01.08 摧坏佛顶陀罗尼卍
namo bhagavato ṣṇīṣāya oṃ viki raṇa dhuna dhuna dhūḥ

M01.09 卍摧毁佛顶陀罗尼卍
namo bhagavato ṣṇīṣāya sarva vighna vidhvaṃ sana karāya ṭroṭaya svāhā

M01.10 卍无能胜佛顶陀罗尼卍
namo bhagavato ṣṇīṣāya sarvatra aparājitāya oṃ śamaya śamaya śānte dānte dharma rāja bhaṣite mahā vidya sarva artha sadhane svāhā

M01.11 卍遍照佛顶陀罗尼卍

M01.12 卍轮王佛顶心陀罗尼卍

M01.13 卍轮王佛顶随心陀罗尼卍

M01.14 卍如来钵陀罗尼卍
namaḥ samanta buddhānāṃ oṃ Ioka pala adhiṣṭhita dhare dharaya mahā dubhava buddha pattra svāhā

M01.16 卍轮王佛顶祈雨陀罗尼卍
namaḥ samanta buddhānāṃ oṃ bhrūṃ sarva nāgāva rṣantu devā śca samantena svāhā

M01.17 卍轮王佛顶止雨陀罗尼卍
namaḥ samanta buddhānāṃ oṃ bhrūṃ caturda kṣu nāgānāṃ pravi śāntu asmiṃ kumbhe sarva duṣṭa nāgānāṃ rundhami svāhā

M01.18 轮王佛顶止非时恶风雨雹陀罗尼
saṃ rundhami vāsukī prasukhā nāgā rājānū yemanu ṣyānāṃ saṃ syaṃ vihe ṭhaya nti namaḥ samanta buddhānāṃ bhrūṃ caturdi kṣunāgānāṃ praviśa asmiṃ kumbhe sarva duṣṭa nāgā nāṃ ruddhami svāhā

M01.19 卍光聚佛顶烧萎花白芥子止雨陀罗尼卍
namaḥ samanta buddhānāṃ apratihata śāsanānāṃ oṃ tathā gato ṣṇīṣa ana valo kita mūrdha tejo raśi hūṃ jvala javla dhaka dhaka vidhaka vidhaka dara dara vidara vidara cchinda cchi nda bhinda bhinda hūṃ hūṃ sarva duṣṭa nāga varṣā sta mbhaya sphaṭ sphaṭ svāhā
